My little pony

director:Jayson Thiessen/James Wootton

My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic is a beloved animated series centered on Twilight Sparkle and her five pony friends in Equestria. Each pony has unique traits and magical abilities, representing values like kindness, loyalty and honesty. The show follows their adventures, emphasizing the power of friendship, and charms kids and families with colorful visuals and heartwarming stories.

Main Characters

Twilight Sparkle

voice:Tara Strong

Twilight Sparkle is the main character in My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. She was once a studious unicorn focused on magic. Guided by Princess Celestia, she befriends five ponies in Equestria, learns the true meaning of friendship, and eventually evolves into an alicorn, guarding her friends and the land.

Pinkie Pie(Pinkamena Diane Pie)

voice:Andrea Libman

Pinkie Pie is a lively Earth Pony in My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. She loves throwing parties, has a cheerful personality and always uses her enthusiasm to bring joy and laughter to her friends in Equestria.

Rarity

voice:Tabitha St Germain

Rarity is a white unicorn with blue - purple mane from My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. As a fashion designer, she owns Carousel Boutique. She represents generosity and excels at finding gems with magic.

Fluttershy

voice:Andrea Libman

Fluttershy is a kind Pegasus in My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. She’s gentle and shy, with a gift for communicating with all kinds of animals. She always cares for creatures around her and spreads kindness quietly.

Rainbow Dash

voice:Ashleigh Ball

Rainbow Dash is a lively pegasus in My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic. She’s blue - maned, super fast, and obsessed with speed. Bold and loyal, she guards Equestria’s skies and values her friends deeply, often helping them bravely.

Apple Jack

voice:Ashleigh Ball

Applejack is a friendly orange earth pony from My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic. She lives on an apple farm, loves farming, and values honesty most. She’s hard - working and always ready to help her friends.
