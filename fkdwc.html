Zootopia

director:Byron Howard

Zootopia is a beloved Disney animated film. It’s set in the eponymous city where mammals of all sizes and species—from tiny shrews to huge elephants—live harmoniously regardless of their natural predator-prey instincts. The story follows Judy Hopps, a determined rabbit who becomes the first rabbit police officer. Teaming up with a witty fox named Nick Wilde, she sets out to solve a missing mammal case. Beyond the thrilling investigation, the film cleverly explores themes of prejudice and dreams, winning hearts of audiences of all ages worldwide.

Main Characters

Judy Hopps

voice:Ginnifer Goodwin

Judy Hopps is the upbeat, determined protagonist of *Zootopia*. A small rabbit from Bunnyburrow, she defies stereotypes to fulfill her dream of becoming Zootopia’s first rabbit police officer. Judy is hard - working, smart and kind - hearted. She teams up with Nick Wilde to solve a tricky missing mammal case, proving that anyone can achieve their goals regardless of size or species through perseverance.

Nick Wilde

voice:Jason Bateman

Nick Wilde is a clever, quick-witted red fox in *Zootopia*. He initially makes a living by tricking others, masking his kindness behind a cynical exterior—this stems from a painful childhood experience of being discriminated against for being a fox. He later teams up with Judy Hopps, the rabbit cop. As they solve a major case together, Nick drops his guard, shows his sense of justice, and finally realizes his dream of becoming a police officer.

Flash

voice:Raymond S. Persi

Flash is a male three-toed sloth who works as a clerk at the Department of Mammal Vehicles in Zootopia. True to his sloth nature, he moves and speaks at an extremely slow pace—even simple actions like blinking or flipping papers take him ages.

Mr.Big

voice:Maurice LaMarche

Mr. Big is a tiny but powerful Arctic shrew and the respected crime boss in Zootopia. He rules the city’s underworld with authority, and everyone fears and respects him. Though he initially seems intimidating, he has a soft spot for his family, especially his daughter. He once held a grudge against Judy and Nick but later helped them solve the missing mammals case, showing a mix of toughness and hidden warmth.
