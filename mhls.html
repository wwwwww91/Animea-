TOM and JERRY

director:William Hanna/Joseph Barbera

Tom and Jerry is a timeless American cartoon. The show focuses on Tom, a cat eager to catch Jerry, his tiny mouse roommate. Their daily life is filled with hilarious chases and clever pranks. Notably, it uses little dialogue, relying on slapstick humor and vivid actions to delight generations of viewers worldwide.

Main Characters

Tom(Thomas)

voice:\

Tom is the grumpy gray house cat in Tom and Jerry. His main obsession is catching the mouse Jerry, but his well-planned schemes always backfire, leaving him in silly, messy predicaments. Though he often gets frustrated, his funny failures make him a memorable character.

Jerry

voice:\

Jerry is a clever, small brown mouse in Tom and Jerry. He’s always one step ahead of Tom the cat. Jerry often plays playful tricks on Tom, turning Tom’s attempts to catch him into funny chaos. His quick wits and courage make him a beloved character.

Spike

voice:\

Spike is a strong bulldog from the cartoon Tom and Jerry. As a key side character, he hates Tom’s chaotic pursuits of Jerry that disturb his naps. He usually punishes Tom severely for the trouble. Notably, Spike is a gentle and protective father, and he will get furious if anyone hurts his little son Tyke.

Nibbles/Tuffy

voice:\

Tuffy is a key character in Tom and Jerry, serving as Jerry’s young nephew. The small gray mouse is innocent yet tricky. He loves joining Jerry’s adventures, and together they make clever plans to tease and outrun Tom the cat. His childlike curiosity and boldness often lead to hilarious chaos, making him a beloved character for audiences.
