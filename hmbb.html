Sbongebob Squarepants

director:Walt Dohrn

SpongeBob SquarePants is a beloved American animated series set in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. Its protagonist is the cheerful yellow sea sponge SpongeBob, who works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab. The show follows his funny daily adventures with best friend Patrick Star, neighbor Squidward, and boss Mr. Krabs. With light - hearted humor and warm stories, it wins the hearts of both kids and adults worldwide, becoming a long - running classic.

Main Characters

Sbongebob Squarepants

voice:Tom Kenny

SpongeBob SquarePants is a cheerful, energetic yellow sea sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea in Bikini Bottom. He works as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab, famous for making delicious Krabby Patties. SpongeBob is super optimistic, loves his job and his best friend Patrick Star deeply. He often gets into funny adventures with Patrick, bringing joy to everyone around him with his childlike enthusiasm.

Squidward Quincy Tentacles

voice:Rodger Bump

Squidward Tentacles is SpongeBob’s grumpy octopus neighbor and colleague at the Krusty Krab. Unlike the hyper SpongeBob, he is introverted, loves playing the clarinet and pursuing art, and dreams of a quiet life. Yet SpongeBob and Patrick’s constant antics always ruin his peace. Despite his irritable exterior, he occasionally shows a hidden soft spot for them.

Patrick Star

voice:Bill Fagerbakke

Patrick Star is SpongeBob’s best friend, a pink, chubby sea star who lives under a rock in Bikini Bottom. He’s rather silly and slow-witted but extremely loyal. Patrick often joins SpongeBob in crazy, funny adventures, and his innocent, carefree personality always adds more laughs. Though not clever, he cherishes his friendship with SpongeBob deeply.

Captain Eugene H.Armor Abs Krabs(Mr.Krabs)

voice:Clancy Brown

Mr. Krabs, full name Eugene H. Krabs, is a red crab and the greedy owner of the Krusty Krab in Bikini Bottom. He adores money more than anything and often comes up with petty schemes to save or earn cash. As SpongeBob’s boss, he values the secret Krabby Patty recipe highly. Despite his stinginess, he cares about his daughter Pearl and occasionally shows a responsible side.
