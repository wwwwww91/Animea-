Toy Story

director:John A. Lasseter

Toy Story, Pixar’s iconic animated franchise, centers on a group of toys that secretly live vivid lives. Led by cowboy Woody and space ranger Buzz Lightyear, the toys experience joys and crises together. Its stories blend humor and warmth, exploring friendship, change and courage, and it has become a timeless hit across all ages.

Main Characters

Sheriff Woody Pride(Woody)

voice:Tom Hanks

Woody is the lead character in Toy Story. A pull-string cowboy doll, he’s loyal to his owner Andy and leads the other toys. Though jealous of Buzz initially, he grows to be Buzz’s best friend, always protecting the toys bravely.

Buzz Lightyear

voice:Tim Allen

Buzz Lightyear is a space ranger action figure in Toy Story. He initially thinks he’s a real hero, not a toy. Later, he befriends Woody and other toys, using his bravery to help them through many adventures.

Jessie

voice:Joan Cusack

Jessie is a brave and passionate pull - string cowgirl doll, first appearing in Toy Story 2. She was modeled after a character on the fictional TV show Woody's Roundup, alongside Sheriff Woody, Prospector Stinky Pete, and Bullseye. Once, Jessie was abandoned by her original owner Emily, which left her with deep - seated sadness. Years of being stored by a toy collector also gave her claustrophobia and abandonment issues.

Slinky Dog

voice:Jim Varney

Slinky Dog is a loyal toy dog in Toy Story. With a metal slinky for a body, he’s friendly and always ready to help Woody and the other toys. His stretchable body often plays a key role in their little adventures.
