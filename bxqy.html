Frozen

director:Walt Dohrn

Frozen is a hit Disney animated movie centered on royal sisters Elsa and Anna. Elsa hides her dangerous ice - controlling powers until an accident freezes their kingdom of Arendelle. Anna embarks on a tough journey to unfreeze the land and mend their bond, with heartwarming themes of love and courage.

Main Characters

Elsa

voice:Spencer Lacey Ganus

Elsa is the elder princess of Arendelle in Frozen. Born with powerful ice - controlling magic, she once feared her powers. Later, she learns to accept and master them, protecting her kingdom and her sister Anna.

Anna

voice:Kristen Bell

Anna is a warm-hearted princess of Arendelle in Frozen. She’s outgoing and brave, devoting herself to mending her relationship with her sister Elsa. Her courage and sincerity finally melt the ice between them.

voice:Jonathan Groff

Kristoff is a rugged ice harvester in Frozen. He lives in the mountains with his loyal reindeer Sven. Kind and reliable, he helps Anna on her journey to find Elsa, showing courage and a warm heart along the way.

Olaf

voice:Josh Gad

Olaf is a naive and cheerful snowman in Frozen, created by Elsa’s magic. He dreams of experiencing summer. Playful and warm - hearted, he always brings joy to Anna and Elsa, and is a loyal friend to them.
